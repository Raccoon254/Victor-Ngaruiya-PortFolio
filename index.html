About Me

We can make it together

I am a 3rd-year law student at Chuka University, where I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Law degree. I have passion for legal profession, particularly criminal and corporate advocacy, as well as technology, telecommunications, and tax law. My interest in criminal law has been driven by a desire to help individuals who have been wronged by the justice system. Through my studies and internships, I have developed a deep understanding of criminal law, and I am always seeking opportunities to apply this knowledge to real-world scenarios. In addition to criminal law, I am also passionate about corporate advocacy. I am intrigued by the legal intricacies that come with representing corporations, and I enjoy researching and analyzing complex legal issues related to corporate governance, mergers and acquisitions, and intellectual property. Moreover, I have a keen interest in technology, telecommunications, and tax law. As technology continues to advance and become integrated into various aspects of our lives, legal issues related to these areas are becoming increasingly important. I am eager to continue learning about the legal implications of emerging technologies and how they can be regulated and managed.

Apart from my academic pursuits, I also offer academic writing and legal research services. I am committed to delivering high-quality work that meets my clients' needs and exceeds their expectations. My experiences and passions have prepared me well for a career in the legal profession, and I look forward to continuing to learn and grow in this field.
